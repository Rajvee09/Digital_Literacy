# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

## General Safety Tips ----
- Never share your OTP, passwords, or UPI PIN with anyone
- Always verify unknown calls, messages, or emails before responding
- Avoid clicking on suspicious or shortened links
- Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible
- Keep your apps and devices updated regularly
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
## UPI & Financial Safety ---
- Never approve unknown “collect requests” on UPI apps
- Remember: entering your UPI PIN means you are sending money
- Always check the receiver’s name before confirming payment
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
## Responsible Online Behavior --
- Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly
- Be cautious while using public Wi-Fi networks
-------------------------------------------------------------------

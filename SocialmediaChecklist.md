# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

##  Do’s

| No. | Do’s |

 1 | Think before posting any content online |
 2 | Maintain a professional and respectful profile |
 3 | Verify information before sharing or reposting |
 4 | Use strong passwords and enable privacy settings |
 5 | Be respectful in comments and online interactions |

---

##  Don’ts

| No. | Don’ts |

 1 | Do not share personal information like phone number or address publicly |
 2 | Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content |
 3 | Do not engage in cyberbullying or unnecessary online arguments |
 4 | Avoid clicking on suspicious links or unknown messages |
 5 | Do not overshare details about your daily activities or location |

Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist
✅ Do’s:
Think before posting any content online
Keep your profiles professional and respectful
Verify information before sharing or reposting
Use strong passwords and enable privacy settings
Be respectful in comments and online interactions
❌ Don’ts:
Do not share personal information like phone number or address publicly
Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content
Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments
Avoid clicking on suspicious links or unknown messages
Do not overshare details about your daily activities or location

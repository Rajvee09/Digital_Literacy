For this task, I used Canva to design a digital literacy awareness infographic. Canva is a user-friendly design tool that provides templates, icons, and layout options, which made it easier to create a visually appealing and organized infographic.

My design presents key aspects of digital literacy relevant to students. It includes a brief explanation of what digital literacy means, along with sections on useful digital tools such as GitHub and LinkedIn, safe internet practices like avoiding suspicious links and protecting passwords, and the importance of maintaining a professional online presence. I also included a short section on email etiquette to highlight proper communication in academic and professional settings. The information is presented using bullet points and icons to ensure clarity and readability.

One challenge I faced was balancing the amount of information with the design layout. Adding too much content made the infographic look crowded, while reducing content risked missing important points. Overall, this task helped me understand how to communicate information effectively through visual design.
